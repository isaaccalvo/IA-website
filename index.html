About Us

We at Island Apparel have been serving beautiful Micronesia since 1991. Our primary services are Screenprinting, embroidery and various amounts of promotional products. We are located in Puerto Rico after Pacific Trading Co. We also have a small store that features locally designed affordable shirts called "Fashion Clicks" that is right next to us.

Our Process

First our customers meet with a sales associate to discuss the details of the merchandise you wish to order, of which it includes the styles, sizes, colors and quatities that you need. After the customer is satified the artwork will be forwarded to the graphic artist. Then the graphic artist will email customer's for final approval. If we don't have a stock item available it will take 4-6 weeks to order, unless an expedited fee is paid then it is 2-3 weeks.

Production

Our production team uses 4 stations for manual printing and 1 fully automatic station as well. If the shirt is a 1 color design, we can produce 1000 finished shirts per day. If the design includes more than 1 color contact us for accurate information on our output.

For embroidery please email/call us for an accurate quote.

Delivery/PickUp

We provide a delivery local service to customers on island upon request. If delivery is off island it will be sent through cargo at the airport if destination in the CNMI or via standard mail if destination is outside of the CNMI.
